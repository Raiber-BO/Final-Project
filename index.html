New Horizons Girls Volleyball Team Advances to Championship Final

The girls volleyball team from New Horizons School has officially secured a spot in the championship finals after an impressive performance throughout the tournament. The team is set to face Santiago Christian School in the final match, which will take place on Thursday, May 7, at Santiago Christian School. Students, teachers, and parents have shown strong support for the team as excitement builds around what is expected to be one of the most competitive matches of the season. The players have been recognized for their discipline, teamwork, and determination, qualities that helped carry them to the final stage of the competition.

Traffic Accident Reported in Front of New Horizons Main Entrance

A traffic accident was reported near the main entrance of New Horizons School, specifically in the area facing the park across from the school. According to preliminary reports, a woman was allegedly chased by a motorcyclist after reportedly colliding with him moments before the incident. Witnesses described a tense situation that quickly drew the attention of students, parents, and drivers nearby. At this time, no official information has been released regarding injuries or potential legal action connected to the accident. Community members continue to encourage drivers to exercise caution around school zones, especially during high-traffic hours when students are arriving or leaving campus.
