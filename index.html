Students, teachers, and parents have shown strong support as excitement builds around what promises to be one of the most competitive matches of the season. Traffic Accident Reported Near New Horizons Main Entrance

Published: May 7, 2026

A traffic incident was reported near the main entrance of New Horizons School, specifically in the area facing the park across from campus. According to preliminary reports, a woman was allegedly chased by a motorcyclist following a collision moments before the incident. Witnesses described a tense situation that quickly drew the attention of students, parents, and passing drivers.
